{% raw %}The gallery contains almost exclusively pictures by Venetian{% endraw %}
{% raw %}masters. The general public will be most interested in the works{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the time of Bellini and the following period, as well as in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}historical pictures by Gentile Bellini and Carpaccio in the XV.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Room, exhibiting a lifelike picture of ancient Venice, the brilliant{% endraw %}
{% raw %}colours of which cause us to forget the poverty of the execution{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and want of inspiration which characterise the individual figures{% endraw %}
{% raw %}as well as the groups. It is instructive to compare these paintings{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with the manner in which Florentine artists of the same epoch{% endraw %}
{% raw %}arranged their groups and described historical events. Attention{% endraw %}
{% raw %}must be drawn to the numerous pictures of Giovanni Bellini (Nos.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}38, 94, and others), representing the assemblage of saints sur¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}rounding the throne of the Madonna ('sacra conversazione'), to the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}beauty of the nude figures, and to the powerful and imposing,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}though not very saintlike male figures. A picture by Boccaccino{% endraw %}
{% raw %}da Cremona (No. 132), a little-known master of the earlier school,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is one of the best of that period. Giorgione's Storm at sea (No. 37){% endraw %}
{% raw %}is of doubtful authenticity, and moreover much damaged. Palma{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Vecchio is not represented here by his best works. On the other{% endraw %}
{% raw %}hand Rocco Marconi's Descent from the Cross (No. 405) is one of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}his finest efforts. Titian's masterpiece , the Assumption of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Virgin (No. 24), requires no comment; the glowing rapture of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}apostles, the jubilant delight of the angels, the beaming bliss of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Madonna, the magnificence of the colouring, cannot fail to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}strike the eye of every beholder. The gallery comprises what is{% endraw %}
