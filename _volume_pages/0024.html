{% raw %}of 'restaurants' of a better class have recently been opened in some{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the larger towns, in which the cookery is generally French. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}waiter is called cameriere, but the approved way of attracting his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}attention is by knocking on the table. If too importunate in his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}recommendations or suggestions he may be checked with the words{% endraw %}

{% raw %}an early dinner is preferable in summer when the midday heat pre¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cludes exertion.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Caffe nero, or coffee without milk, is usually drunk (10-15 c. per{% endraw %}
