{% raw %}scribes as 'a state in which the noblest institutions prosper when the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}prince is a good man; in which the greatest horrors are possible when{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the prince cannot govern himself; a state which has everywhere thriven{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in Mohammedan countries, but rarely in the middle ages in other Christian{% endraw %}
{% raw %}countries besides this'. In 1494 when Lodovico il Moro induced Charles{% endraw %}
{% raw %}VIII. of France to undertake a campaign against Naples, he inaugurated{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a new period in the history of Italy. Since that time Italy has at once{% endraw %}
{% raw %}been the battlefield and the prey of the great powers of Europe. Lodo¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}vico himself, after having revolted against France and been defeated at{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Novara in 1500, terminated his career in a French dungeon. In 1525 the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}battle of Pavia constituted Charles V. arbiter of the fortunes of Italy. In{% endraw %}
{% raw %}1535, after the death of .the last Sforza, he invested his son, Philip II. of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Spain, with the duchy of Milan. In 1713 the Spanish supremacy was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}followed by the Austrian in consequence of the War of Succession. On{% endraw %}
{% raw %}four occasions (1733, 1745, 1796, and 1800) the French took possession of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Milan, and the Napoleonic period at length swept away the last relics of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}its mediaeval institutions. Although Napoleon annexed the whole of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Piedmont, Genoa, Parma, Tuscany, and Rome (about 36,000 sq. M. of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Italian territory) to France, the erection of a kingdom of Italy contrib¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}uted materially to arouse a national spirit of patriotism. This kingdom{% endraw %}
{% raw %}embraced Lombardy, Venice, S. Tyrol, Istria, the greater part of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Emilia, and the Marches (about 32,000 sq. M.). Milan was the capital,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and Napoleon was king, but was represented by his stepson Engine{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Beauharnais. The Austrian Supremacy, which was restored in 1815, proved{% endraw %}
{% raw %}irreconcilable with the national aspirations of the people. By the Peace{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of Zurich (10th Nov. 1859), Lombardy, with the exception of the district{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of Mantua, was ceded to Napoleon III., and by him to Sardinia.{% endraw %}
