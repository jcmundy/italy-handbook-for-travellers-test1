{% raw %}The road to Colico at first traverses vineyards ; farther on , the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}effects of the inundations of the Maira, and its tributary the Liro,{% endraw %}

{% raw %}65 M. Riva the road reaches the Lago di Riva, or di Mezzola,{% endraw %}

{% raw %}to cross by boat. This piece of water originally formed the N. bay{% endraw %}
