{% raw %}consistent. The liberals called him the 'Re Tentenna1 (the vacillating),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}while in 1843 he himself described his position as being 'between the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}daggers of the Carbonari and the chocolate of the Jesuits'. On 6th{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Jan. 1848 Count Cavour made the first public demand for the establish¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ment of a constitution , and on the 7th Feb. the king , half in despair,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}yielded to the popular desires. The insurrection in Lombardy at length{% endraw %}
{% raw %}induced him to become the champion of national independence, and to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}give vent to his old enmity against Austria (23rd March), but one year{% endraw %}
{% raw %}later his career terminated with his defeat at Novara (23rd. March, 1849).{% endraw %}

{% raw %}d. 9th Jan. 1878) finally to give effect to the national wishes of Italy.{% endraw %}
