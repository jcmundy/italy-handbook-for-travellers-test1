{% raw %}On the N. side of the town is situated —{% endraw %}
{% raw %}*S. Frediano (PL 2; D, 2), a basilica of the 7th cent., founded{% endraw %}
{% raw %}by the Lombard kings Bertharic and Cunibert, in honour of St. Fri-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}gidianus, an Irishman, who was bishop of Lucca in 560-78. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}present facade was erected in the 12th cent, on the site of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}former apse ; the Ascension in mosaic of the same period with which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}it is adorned was restored in 1827. The nave was originally flanked{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with double aisles, the outer of which have been converted into{% endraw %}
{% raw %}chapels. Most of the 22 columns are ancient.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The *Cappella di S. Agostino (2nd to ihe left) contains two old *Frescoes{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Ridolfi. On the ceiling God the Father, surrounded by angels , prophets,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and sibyls; in the lunette to the left the Entombment; below it, to the left,{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Nativity and Adoration of the Magi; on the right, S. Frigidiano miraculously{% endraw %}
