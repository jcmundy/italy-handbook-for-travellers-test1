{% raw %}No town in Italy has undergone such marked improvement as Milan{% endraw %}

{% raw %}the highest rank in the kingdom. Sculpture is here carried on to such{% endraw %}
{% raw %}an extent as to have become almost a special industry. The Milanese{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Sculptors take great pride in their technical skill, and in effective{% endraw %}
{% raw %}imitations of nature. Among the best known sculptors are Magni, Peduzzi,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Tandardini, Barzaghi, Argenti, Calvi, and Baccaglia. — Painting is{% endraw %}

{% raw %}or Garibaldi, Sempione, etc.), have sprung up.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}The celebrated **Cathedral (PL E, F, 5), dedicated 'Mariae{% endraw %}
{% raw %}NascentV, as the inscription on the facade announces, and as the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}gilded statue on the tower over the dome also indicates, erected in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Gothic style, is regarded by the Milanese as the eighth wonder{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the world, and is, next to St. Peter's at Rome and the cathedral{% endraw %}
{% raw %}at Seville, the largest church in Europe. The interior is 159 yds.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in length, 61 yds. in breadth; nave 155 ft. in height, 17 yds. in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}breadth. The dome is 220 ft. in height, the tower 360 ft. above{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the pavement. The roof is adorned with 98 Gothic turrets, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the exterior with upwards of 2000 statues in marble. The structure,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which was founded by the splendour-loving Gian Galeazzo Visconti{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Heinrich von Gmilnd are named as the architects of the cath¬{% endraw %}

{% raw %}appears to have been associated in the superintendence of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}building. The Renaissance ornamentation of the facade (doors and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}windows) was executed partly by Pellegrino Tibaldi in the middle{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the 16th cent., and partly at a still later date. In 1805 Napoleon{% endraw %}
{% raw %}caused the works to be resumed , and the tower over the dome to{% endraw %}
