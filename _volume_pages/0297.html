{% raw %}Giov. Simone Mayr (d. 1845). — The facade of the adjoining "Cappella{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Colleoni (shown by the sagrestano of the church), in the early-Renaissance{% endraw %}
{% raw %}style, is lavishly adorned with coloured marbles and sculpturing. In the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}interior, which has been much altered, is the monument of the founder{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Bartolommeo Colleoni (d. 1475; p. 273), by G. Ant. Amadeo, which is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}deservedly considered one of the best Renaissance sculptures in Lombardy.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The reliefs represent the Bearing of the Cross, Crucifixion, and Descent{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from the Cross; below runs a frieze of Cupids, above which are the An¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}nunciation, the 'Nativity, and the Magi; and on the summit is the gilded{% endraw %}
{% raw %}equestrian statue of Colleoni; adjacent, the much smaller, but beautifully{% endraw %}
{% raw %}executed monument of his daughter Medea. Above the altar, to the right,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}are some fine sculptures ; to the left, a Holy Family by Angelica Kaufmann.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The adjoining Cathedral (PL C, 2, 3) was built from the designs{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of C. Fontana in 1689 on the site of an earlier edifice. At the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}first altar to the left is a Madonna and saints by G. B. Moroni, a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}pupil of Moretto; the choir contains a Madonna by Savoldo. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}adjacent Baptistery, by Giovanni da Campione (1340), recently{% endraw %}
{% raw %}restored, is best viewed from the passage leading to the sacristy,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in which are three pictures by Lorenzo Lotto.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}On the slope of the hill, in the street leading to the lower town,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is situated the Accademia Carrara (PL 11; E, 2), a school of art{% endraw %}
{% raw %}containing a picture-gallery (Galleria Carrara and Galleria Lochis;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}open to the public daily from 30th Aug. to 18th Sept.; during the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}rest of the year on the 1st Sun. and 3rd Thurs. of each month;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}shown at other times on application to the custodian).{% endraw %}
