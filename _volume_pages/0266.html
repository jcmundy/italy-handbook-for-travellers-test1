{% raw %}canton of Ticino, with 6000 inhab., is charmingly situated on{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the lake of the same name, and enjoys quite an Italian climate (the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}agave blooming here in the open air). It is a very pleasant place{% endraw %}

{% raw %}guished by its comparatively low elevation above the sea. The{% endraw %}
