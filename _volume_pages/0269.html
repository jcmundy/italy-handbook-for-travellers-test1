{% raw %}1 64 Route 24. PORLEZZA.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}coolness may be obtained here ('Asti' recommended), and there is also{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a brewery.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Excursion to the "Grotto of Osteno, see below.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Beyond Lugano the road gradually winds upwards to the W.,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}turns S. past the small Lake of Muzzano (1100 ft.), crosses the Agno,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}leads through the (3 M.) village of that name (967 ft.), and a short{% endraw %}
{% raw %}distance farther reaches the W. arm of the Lake of Lugano. Near{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Magliaso the lake is quitted, but another of its bays is touched near{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(3 M.) Ponte Tresa. This bay, which is so completely enclosed by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mountains as apparently to form a distinct lake; is connected with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Lake of Lugano by a narrow channel only. The Tresa, which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}here emerges from the lake and forms the frontier between Switzer¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}land and Italy, falls into the Lago Maggiore, 3/4 M. to the S.W. of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Luino. It is crossed by a bridge at Ponte Tresa.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Steamboat from Lugano to Ponte Tresa in l3/4hr., a pleasant trip{% endraw %}
{% raw %}round the Monte S. Salvatore. The stations are Campione (L), Bissone{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(1.; p. 161), Melide (r.; p. 161), Brusin-Arsizio (1.), Morcote (i\; p. 163),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Porto-Ceresio (1.; omnibus in I72 hr. to Varese, p. 165), Brusin-Piano (L),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and lastly Ponte Tresa.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The road follows the course of the Tresa as far as the Italian{% endraw %}
{% raw %}frontier at Fornace and Fornasette, where luggage is examined;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}it then descends, and soon affords a view of the Lago Maggiore.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}15 M. Luino, see p. 169.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}2. From Menaggio by Porlezza to Lugano.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Omnibus from Menaggio to Porlezza in 2 hrs. (fare 3fr. 60 c); one-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}horse carriage 6-8 fr.; two-horse 12-14 fr. From Porlezza to Lugano{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Steamboat (twice daily there and back; Tuesdays three times) in one{% endraw %}
{% raw %}hour (fare 2V2 or lfr. in silver); boat with one rower 7, with two 12,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with three 1672fr.; bargaining necessary.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The journey from Menaggio to Porlezza (9 M.) is also recom¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mended to pedestrians, as the road leads through a succession of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}imposing and attractive mountain-scenes. The Villa Vigoni(p. 156){% endraw %}
{% raw %}lies to the right of the road (N.). The retrospect from the height{% endraw %}
{% raw %}near Croce, 2 M. from Menaggio, is delightful. Towards the W.,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}on the left the Monte Crocione, and opposite to us the Monte Gal-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}biga (5630 ft.) rise precipitously from the lake. The road then de¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}scends to the small Lago del Piano and the village of Tavordo.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Thence to Porlezza II/4 M. more.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Porlezza (Albergo del Lago), with 1200 inhab., is situated at{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the N. end of the Lake of Lugano. Attempts at extortion are{% endraw %}
{% raw %}frequently made here by the fraternity who prey upon travellers.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Soon after Porlezza is quitted, the Monte S. Salvatore (p. 163){% endraw %}
{% raw %}becomes conspicuous to the S.W. The steamer touches at Osteno{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(Inn on the lake), on the left.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The interesting "Grotto of Osteno may easily be visited from Lugano{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with the aid of the steamboat bound for Porlezza (disembarking at{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Osteno, and returning by the next boat). The grotto is 7 min. from the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}landing-place; the boatman is to be found in the village. The mouth{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the gorge, in which there are two small waterfalls, is near a pro¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}jecting rock. Visitors embark in a small boat and enter the grotto{% endraw %}
