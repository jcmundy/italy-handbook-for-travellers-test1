{% raw %}The cars start from the Stazione Erba, Foro Bonaparte (PL C, 4), to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which another line runs from the Piazza del Duomo. Seven trains (en¬{% endraw %}

{% raw %}The principal station on this route is Qfa nr-) Saronno (Albergo{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Madonna), a large village on the Lura, with 5000 inhabitants.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The *Santuario della Beata Vergine , a celebrated pilgrimage-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}church here, was built at different times between the end of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}15th and the end of the 17th cent., chiefly in a pompous rococo{% endraw %}
{% raw %}style. It contains a series of admirable frescoes.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The paintings in the interior of the dome represent a concert of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}angels, and are by Gaudenzio Ferrari. Round the drum are several wooden{% endraw %}
{% raw %}statues by Andrea Fusina Milanese. The frescoes immediately below the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}drum are by Lanini, those in the next section by Cesare da Sesto and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Bernard. Luini (SS. Rochus and Sebastian). The remaining frescoes are{% endraw %}
{% raw %}all by Luini, who, as the story goes, sought an asylum in the sanctuary{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of Saronno after killing a man in self-defence, and had to work at the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bidding of the monks. In the passage leading to the choir are depicted{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Marriage of the Virgin and Christ among the doctors; in the choir{% endraw %}
{% raw %}itself, the "Adoration of the Magi and the Presentation in the Temple.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Above, in the panels and lunettes, are Sibyls, Evangelists, and Church{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Fathers. A small apse built out from the choir contains paintings of "S.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Apollonia to the right, and *S. Catharine to the left, each with an angel.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}90 c). The intermediate stations are Caronno, Garbagnate, Bollate, Novate,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and Bovisa (p. 149). — A Tramway also runs from Saronno to Tradate{% endraw %}
