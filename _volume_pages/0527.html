{% raw %}their position indicates, and are therefore all worthy of careful in¬{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Lippi's Madonna and two angels (1307), and four works of Sandro{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Botticelli: a round picture of the Madonna (1267 bis), the Ador¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ation of the Magi (1286), so much extolled by Vasari, and, as spe¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cimens of other subjects, his Venus (39), and his Calumniation of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Apelles (1182). Filippino Lippi's Madonna and saints (1268) at¬{% endraw %}
