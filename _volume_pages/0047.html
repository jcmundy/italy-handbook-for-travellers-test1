{% raw %}A taste for beauty of detail, coeval with the realistic tendency of{% endraw %}

{% raw %}There is no doubt that the Renaissance palaces (among which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}that of Urbino, mentioned in vol. ii. of this Handbook, has always{% endraw %}

{% raw %}associations connected with the mediaeval cathedrals, bear ample{% endraw %}
{% raw %}testimony to the ability of their builders. The churches of Northern{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Italy in particular are worthy of examination. The first early Re¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}naissance work constructed in this part of the country was the facade{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the Certosa of Pavia, a superb example of decorative architecture.{% endraw %}
