{% raw %}The *Cathedral (PL F, 4), II Duomo, or La Cattedrale di S.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Maria del Fiore, so called from the lily which figures in the arms{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of Florence, was erected in 1294-1462 on the site of the earlier{% endraw %}
