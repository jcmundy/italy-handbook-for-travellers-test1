{% raw %}Como (705 ft.), the capital of a province, with 11,000 inhab.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(commune 25,600), and large silk-factories, the birthplace of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}elder and younger Pliny and of the electrician and philosopher Volta{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(d. 1826; whose Statue by P. Marchesi is on the W. side of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}town near the quay), lies at the S. end of the S.W. arm of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Lake of Como , and is enclosed by an amphitheatre of mountains.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The *Cathedral, begun in the Lombard-Gothic style in 1396,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and altered in the Renaissance style by Tommaso Rodari (choir,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}transept, outside of nave) in 1513-21, is built entirely of marble,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and is one of the best in N. Italy. The dome is modern. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}greater part of the beautiful plastic ornamentation is by Rodari{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and other contemporary Lombard artists. Over the fine N. portal{% endraw %}
{% raw %}are reliefs (adoration of the Magi) and statuettes (Mary with S. Ab-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bondio, St. Protus, etc.). At the sides of the main entrance are{% endraw %}
{% raw %}statues of the elder and the younger Pliny, erected in 1498.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Interior. The gaudy vaulting, restored in 1838 at a cost of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}600,000 fr., destroys the effect of the fine proportions, which resemble{% endraw %}
{% raw %}those of the Certosa near Pavia (p. 176). The windows of the portal{% endraw %}
{% raw %}contain good modern stained glass, representing the history of S. Abbon-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}dio; there are others to the right of the entrance and in the choir. — To{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the right of the entrance is the monument of Cardinal Tolomeo Gallio, a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}benefactor of the town, erected in 1861. Farther on, to the right, 3rd{% endraw %}
{% raw %}* Altar, di S. Abbondio, with handsome wood-carving, and scenes from the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}life of the saint; adjoining the "Adoration of the Magi, by Bern. Luini, and{% endraw %}

{% raw %}a Madonna by B. Luini. In the N. Transept the Altare del Crocefisso of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}1498, with a fine statue of St. Sebastian. In the Choir the Apostles, by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Pompeo Marchesi. The Sacristy contains pictures by Guido Reni, Paolo{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Veronese, etc. In the Left Aisle, the altar of the Mater Dolorosa with an{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Entombment by Tommaso Rodari (1498). At the Altare di S. Giuseppe :{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Nativity; St. Joseph, a statue by P. Marchesi, and a basrelief below,{% endraw %}

{% raw %}cent XL (Odescalchi) and Carlo Ravelli, bishop of Como.{% endraw %}
