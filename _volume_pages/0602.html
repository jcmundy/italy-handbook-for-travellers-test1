{% raw %}(K.) Vallombrosa. A visit to this celebrated monastery may{% endraw %}
{% raw %}be accomplished from Florence in one day; it is advisable, however,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to start on the previous evening and pass the night at Pelago.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}If the excursion includes the Casentino Valley and the monasteries{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of Camaldoli and Alvernia, three or four days will be required.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Pontassieve (in 55 min. ; fares 2 fr. 30, 1 fr. 55, 1 fr. 10 c). From{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the central station near S. Maria Novella the train performs the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}circuit of the city, and stops at the station near Porta Croce, which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}may be more conveniently situated for some travellers than the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}principal station. It then skirts the right bank of the Arno. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}valley soon contracts. 8 M. Compiobbi, a small village, lies in a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}richly-cultivated district, above which rise barren heights.{% endraw %}
