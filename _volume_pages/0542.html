{% raw %}From the Piazza della Signoria the busy Via dei Calzajoli{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(PL E, 4, 5) leads towards theN. to the Piazza del Duomo.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The Via di Porta Rossa, the first side-street to the left, leads to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Mercato Nuovo, once the market for silks and jewelry, where{% endraw %}
{% raw %}straw and woollen wares are now sold. The market is adorned with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a good copy of the ancient boar in bronze, by Pietro Tacca; the ar¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cades are by Bern. Tasso (1547). Farther on in the same street,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to the left, is the Palazzo Davanzati, a building of the 14th cent.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}■— In the third street diverging to the right from the Via Calzajoli,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}on the left side, beyond the first cross-street, is the house in which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Dante was born (Via S. Martino 2; PL 46). It has been recently{% endraw %}
{% raw %}restored, and provided with a memorial tablet bearing the inscription{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'in questa casa degli Alighierinacque il divino poeta'. (The traveller{% endraw %}
{% raw %}interested in historical research should observe the numerous me¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}morial-tablets in various parts of the town, recording important{% endraw %}
{% raw %}events in the annals of Florence.){% endraw %}
{% raw %}In the Via Calzajoli, on the left, rises the church of *Or San{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Michele (PL 31; D, 4), or originally S. Michele in Orto, as it was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}called from a plot of grass once here, which was converted into a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}grain-market in 1284, and covered with a roof supporting a corn-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}magazine arid adorned with a highly revered statue of the Virgin{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and another of the Archangel Michael. In 1336 it was resolved to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}erect a 'Palatium', containing an oratory below, and a corn-exchange{% endraw %}
{% raw %}above. The work was at length begun in 1350, in the Gothic style,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}under the supervision of Taddeo Gaddi, and completed in 1412.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The external decoration of the edifice with statues was undertaken{% endraw %}
{% raw %}by the twelve guilds, whose armorial bearings, some of them by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Luca della Robbia, are placed above the niches.{% endraw %}
