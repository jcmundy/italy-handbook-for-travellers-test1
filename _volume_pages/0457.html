{% raw %}painted in 1513 on the commission of Cardinal Lorenzo Pucci for{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the church of S. Giovanni in Monte (p. 316). It was at Paris from{% endraw %}
{% raw %}1796 to 1815.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'The youthful and beautiful patron saint of music has just ceased{% endraw %}
{% raw %}playing the organ to her friends, and a heavenly echo falls upon their{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ears. Six angels, resting on the edge of a cloud, have caught up the melody{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and continue it by singing. Raphael's painting depicts the impression{% endraw %}
{% raw %}produced by the celestial music. The saints on earth are silent in pre¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sence of the heavenly choir. St. Cecilia lets her hands rest mechanically{% endraw %}
{% raw %}upon the organ, but, with bead and eyes turned upwards, listens entranced{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to the song. St. Paul, to her left, is differently affected. Sunk in deep{% endraw %}
{% raw %}meditation, he also seems completely oblivious of the actual world. In{% endraw %}
{% raw %}pleasing contrast to these two figures, Mary Magdalene, who stands on the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}right of St. Cecilia and holds a box of ointment in her hand, shows her{% endraw %}
{% raw %}delight simply and openly. ... In the second line stand SS. John the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Evangelist and Augustine (or Petronius?). ... A crowning touch is added{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to the careful distribution of the figures and well-balanced discrimination{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of expression by the harmonious arrangement of the colours. The strongest{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and most intense tone is afforded by the yellow tunic of St. Cecilia,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}embroidered with gold; in the St. Paul the predominant tint is the red{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of his mantle, relieved by the green under-garment; the Magdalene's dress{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is of a violet colour. The toning down and blending of the ground-tints{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is effected through the two saints in the background, who thus fulfill the{% endraw %}
