{% raw %}Opposite St. Mark's, to the S.W., rises the isolated square{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'^Campanile (II Campanile di S. Marco), 322 ft. in height, which is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}always open to the public (doorkeeper 15 c. for each pers. on enter¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ing). It was founded in 911, several times restored, and finally{% endraw %}
{% raw %}completed in 1511, the upper part and the spire having been con¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}structed by Bartolommeo Buon. The ascent by a winding inclined{% endraw %}
{% raw %}plane of 38 bends, and finally by a few steps, is easy and well-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lighted. The watchman at the summit is provided with a telescope{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and opens the door to the second gallery for a trifling gratuity. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}*View comprises the city, the Lagune (comp. p. 237), the Alps, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}part of the Adriatic; W. the Monti Euganei near Padua (p. 299),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}rising above the Lagune; E. in clear weather the Istrian Mts., ris¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ing above the Adriatic, a magnificent spectacle towards sunset. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ascent of the campanile is recommended to the traveller, both for a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}preliminary survey, and as an appropriate termination to his visit to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Venice. —The Loggetta, or vestibule, on the E. side of the campa¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}nile, erected by Sansovino in 1540 and lately restored, once served{% endraw %}
{% raw %}as a waiting-room for the procurators, whose office it was, during{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the sessions of the great Council, to command the guards. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bronze statues of Peace, Apollo, Mercury, and Pallas, and the re¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}liefs on the coping by Sansovino, and also the Bronze Doors, cast{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in 1750, deserve inspection. The interior contains a group of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Madonna with the Infant Christ and John the Baptist, in gilded{% endraw %}
{% raw %}terracotta, by Sansovino.{% endraw %}
