{% raw %}The Place des Terreaux(P1. D,3), in which the H6tel-de-Ville{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and the Museum are situated, occupies the site of the Ara Augusti{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(see above), which stood at the point where the Rhone and Saone{% endraw %}
{% raw %}formerly mingled their waters. Here Richelieu caused the youthful{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Marquis de Cinq-Mars, who for a short period was the favourite of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Louis XIII., and his partisan De Thou to be executed as traitors,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}12th Sept., 1642. Numerous victims of the Revolution perished{% endraw %}
{% raw %}here by the guillotine in 1794, after which the more wholesale{% endraw %}
{% raw %}system of drowning and shooting was introduced. The Hotel-de-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Ville (PI. 62), a handsome edifice built by Maupin in 1647-55,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}has been recently restored.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The Palais des Beaux Arts, or Museum (PI. 69 ; D, 3), is open{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to visitors from 11 to 4, on Sundays and Thursdays gratis, on other{% endraw %}
{% raw %}days for a gratuity (20-25 c. in each of the different sections).{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The ground-floor contains Roman antiquities (altars, inscriptions,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}statues in stone and bronze, plaster casts, vases, etc.), the marble busts of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}several distinguished natives of Lyons, and a collection of coins and seals.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The Picture Gallery is on the first floor. Salle des Anciens Maitres :{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in the centre four Roman mosaics, representing Orpheus, Cupid and Pan,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and the games of the circus. Among the pictures may be mentioned:{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Terburg, The Message; Palma Oiovane, Scourging of Christ; ''Pielro{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Perugino, Ascension, one of this master's finest works, painted in 1495{% endraw %}
{% raw %}for the cathedral of Perugia, and presented to the town by Pius VII.;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Sebastian del Piombo, Christ reposing; Guercino, Circumcision; Perugino,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}SS. James and Gregory; *01d copy of Diirer^s Madonna and Child{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bestowing bouquets of roses on the Emp. Maximilian and his consort, a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}celebrated picture containing numerous figures, painted by the master for{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the German merchants at Venice in 1506 (p. 264; original at Prague).{% endraw %}
{% raw %}There are also works by Rubens and Jordaens, A. del Sarto, the Car-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}racci, and others. — On the floor above is the Galerie des Peintres{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Lyonnais : Bonnefond, Portrait of Jacquard, inventor of the improved{% endraw %}
{% raw %}loom, born at Lyons in 1752, died 1834; Paul and Hippolyte Flandrin,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and others.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Sun. and Thurs.), founded in 1858; the specimens in illustration{% endraw %}
