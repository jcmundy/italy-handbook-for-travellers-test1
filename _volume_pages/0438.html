{% raw %}The *Cathedral (#. Giorgio; PI. 1; D, 5) has an imposing facade{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with three series of round arches, one above the other, an ad¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mirable example of the Lombard style. The lower part of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}front and the lateral facades date from 1135; the upper part is of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the 13th cent., the sculptures of the 13th and 14th. The projecting{% endraw %}
{% raw %}portal, enriched with sculptures and four lions, was added at a later{% endraw %}
{% raw %}period; the reliefs, however, are of an earlier date.{% endraw %}
