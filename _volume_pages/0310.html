{% raw %}From Brescia to (6 M.) Palazzolo, see p. 185. Our line here{% endraw %}
{% raw %}diverges to the N.W. Stat. Capriolo, then (24 M.) Paratico,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}situated on the left bank of the Oglio, which here issues from the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Lago d'Iseo. On the opposite bank of the river lies Sarnico (*Leone{% endraw %}
{% raw %}d'Oro), a pretty, quaint-looking town with walls and towers, con¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}nected with Paratico by a bridge. Near the town is the Villa Mon-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tecchio, commanding a superb view. Sarnico is the terminus of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}steamboats plying on the Lago d'Iseo.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The *Lago d'Iseo (Lacus Sebinus, 620 ft. above the sea-level),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}about 15 M. in length from N. to S., about 1000 ft. deep in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}centre, and averaging I72 M. in breadth, somewhat resembles an{% endraw %}
{% raw %}S in form. The Oglio enters the lake between Pisogne and Lovere{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and emerges from it near Sarnico. The scenery vies in beauty{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with that of the Lago di Garda, the soil is admirably cultivated,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and the vegetation of a luxuriant, southern character. The Mezz-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Isola, an island I72 M. in length, consisting of a lofty ridge{% endraw %}
{% raw %}descending precipitously on the E. side, rises picturesquely and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}boldly in the middle of the lake. Along the E. bank of the lake,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from Iseo to Pisogne (see below), runs the high-road from Brescia{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to Milan, which is little inferior in boldness to that on the banks{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the Lake of Como. It is carried through a number of galleries{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and supported by solid masonry, and commands magnificent views of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the lake and of its banks.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}From Sarnico the Steamer at first steers to the W. to Iseo{% endraw %}

{% raw %}dustries are oil-pressing, dyeing, and silk-spinning. We then{% endraw %}
{% raw %}turn to the N. and call at Sulzano and at the fishing-village of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Peschiera, on the Mezz' Isola. To the S. of Peschiera lies the islet{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of S. Paolo. The next station (E.) is Sale-Marasino, consisting of{% endraw %}
