{% raw %}ideals in the 13th century is one of the most interesting phenomena{% endraw %}

{% raw %}a Bacchus vase still preserved in the Campo Santo of that city{% endraw %}

{% raw %}to determine. His sculptures on the pulpits in the Baptistery of{% endraw %}

{% raw %}taste for poetry, which had already strongly manifested itself, was{% endraw %}
