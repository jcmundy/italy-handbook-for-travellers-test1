{% raw %}46 Route 6. ARCO. From Innsbruck{% endraw %}
{% raw %}From Trent to Riva on the Lago di Garda , 26 M. Omnibus once{% endraw %}
{% raw %}daily in 6 hrs., starting usually at 9 a.m., fare 2 fl.; carriage with one{% endraw %}
{% raw %}horse 9, with two horses 15 fl.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}This route is far preferable to the direct railway-journey to Verona{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(see p. 47) on account of the charming scenery of the Lago di Garda.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The traveller from Botzen, whose time is limited, may shorten the route{% endraw %}
{% raw %}by taking the railway to Mori (p. 47), and driving thence to (10 M.) Riva{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(see p. 187). Omnibus thrice daily in 272 hrs., fare 90 kr. (coupe 1 fl.).{% endraw %}
