{% raw %}works of Van Eyck's School are a small Madonna by Memling{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(703), and an Entombment by Roger van der Weyden (795). The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Man praying (769) is either by Memling or Hugo van der Goes,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and the two portraits (749) are variously attributed to the latter{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and to Petrus Cristus, Among the principal German masters,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Diirer is represented by an Adoration of the Magi (in the Tribuna),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a portrait of his father (766), two heads of Apostles (768, 777),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and an unattractive Madonna (851). Holbein's portrait of Richard{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Southwell, dating from 1537 (No. 765), is an admirable work, but{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the portrait of himself, which was originally a slightly coloured{% endraw %}
{% raw %}charcoal drawing only, has been painted over and much altered.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The Netherlands School of the 17th cent, is also represented by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}several excellent works. Among those by Rubens are a small{% endraw %}
{% raw %}picture of Venus and Adonis, with a landscape by J. Brueghel{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(812), the portrait of his first wife (197), and two pictures of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}scenes from the life of Henry IV. (140, 147). The best of Rem¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}brandt's works preserved here are the two portraits of himself{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(451, 452), and a small Holy Family (922). The Dutch genre-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}painters have also enriched the gallery with several important and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}well-preserved works, such as Ger. Dow's Apple-woman (926),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and the Schoolmaster (786), Fr. Mieris' Charlatan (854), and the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}large family-portrait (981), G. Metsu's Lady and hunter (972), and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Jan Steen's Family feast (977). Among the portraits of the paint¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ers those by the Netherlands masters unquestionably occupy the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}highest rank. The modern portraits of the painters placed here show{% endraw %}
{% raw %}how sadly this branch of art has fallen off since the 17th century.{% endraw %}
