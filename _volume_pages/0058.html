{% raw %}was the Last Judgment in the Sistine Chapel, his next the erection{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the scanty fragments of the tomb of Pope Julius. His last years{% endraw %}
{% raw %}were mainly devoted to architecture (St. Peters).{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Amateurs will best be enabled to render justice to Michael{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Angelo by first devoting their attention to his earlier works,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}among which in the province of sculpture the group of the Pieta{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in St. Peter's occupies the highest rank. The statues of Bacchus{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and David (at Florence) likewise do not transgress the customary{% endraw %}
{% raw %}precepts of the art of the Renaissance. Paintings of Michael{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Angelo's earlier period are rare; the finest, whether conceived{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in the midst of his youthful studies, or in his maturer years, is un¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}questionably the ceiling-painting in the Sistine. The architectural{% endraw %}
{% raw %}arrangement of the ceiling, and the composition of the several{% endraw %}
{% raw %}pictures are equally masterly; the taste and discrimination of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}painter and sculptor are admirably combined. In God the Father,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Michael Angelo produced a perfect type of its kind; he under¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}stood how to inspire with dramatic life the abstract idea of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}act of creation, which he conceived as motion in the prophets{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and sibyls. Notwithstanding the apparent monotony of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fundamental intention (foreshadowing of the Redemption), a great{% endraw %}
{% raw %}variety of psychological incidents are displayed and embodied in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}distinct characters. Lastly, in the so-called Ancestors of Christ,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the forms represented are the genuine emanations of Michael{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Angelo's genius, pervaded by his profound and sombre senti¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ments, and yet by no means destitute of gracefulness and beauty.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The decorative figures also which he designed to give life to his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}architectural framework are wonderfully beautiful and spirited.{% endraw %}
