{% raw %}The Interior, in the form of a Latin cross, with nave and aisles{% endraw %}
{% raw %}91 yds. in length, transept 39, and nave 28 yds. in width, has a clerestory{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(with large windows and rich tracery) over the aisles and carried across{% endraw %}

{% raw %}the 14th cent., especially in the arches of the transept. Above the aisles{% endraw %}
{% raw %}are galleries. The old frescoes on the vaulting were restored in 1858.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}by Pandolfo di Ugolino da Pisa. — 1st Altar on the right, Nativity by Passi-{% endraw %}

{% raw %}to the Pisans, but was carried off by the inhabitants of Lucca (not shown{% endraw %}
