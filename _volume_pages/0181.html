{% raw %}In the Piazza dell' Annunziata (PL E, 2) is the Capuchin{% endraw %}
{% raw %}church of *S. Annunziata (PL 10), erected in 1587, with a portal{% endraw %}
{% raw %}borne by marble columns, the brick facade being otherwise un¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}finished. It is a cruciform structure with a dome, the vaulting{% endraw %}

{% raw %}richly gilded and painted. This is the most sumptuous church at{% endraw %}
