{% raw %}A few paces from this church is the Piazza Grande (PL C,{% endraw %}

{% raw %}In this piazza is situated the Palazzo Pubblico (PL 10 ; C, 3),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}formerly Ducale, begun in 1578 from designs by Ammanati, but{% endraw %}
{% raw %}still incomplete. On the first floor is a Picture Gallery (open daily{% endraw %}
{% raw %}10-2, closed on Sundays and festivals).{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Madonna della Misericordia, with portraits of the Moncalieri family, of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}1515 (formerly in S. Romano): — 'The classic movement of the principal{% endraw %}
