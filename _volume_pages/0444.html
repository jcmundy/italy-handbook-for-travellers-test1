{% raw %}The situation of Bologna is healthy. The mean annual temperature is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}2° Fahr. lower than that of Florence. The drinking-water is brought{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from Sasso, a station on the railway to Florence (p. 333), by an old Roman{% endraw %}
{% raw %}aqueduct repaired by the engineer Zannoni. — The town is sometimes{% endraw %}
{% raw %}called iBologna la grassa\ owing to its reputation for wealth and good-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}living. The neighbourhood produces tolerable wines and excellent fruit.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The grapes are delicious; the yellow Uva Paradisa is a kind which may{% endraw %}
{% raw %}be kept a considerable time — The once favourite lap-dogs of Bologna are{% endraw %}
{% raw %}now almost extinct. Soap, maccaroni, and liqueurs ('bebita1) are among{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the most esteemed commodities of the place.{% endraw %}
