{% raw %}perature is 10-15° Fahr. higher than that of Paris, summer temperature{% endraw %}
{% raw %}5-10° lower. Frost is rare. The Mistral, or N.W. wind, the scourge of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Provence, is seldom felt, being intercepted by the Montagnes du Var and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}de l'Esterel. The E. wind, however, which generally prevails in spring,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is trying to delicate persons, and the clouds of dust raised by it in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Promenade des Anglais have given rise to numerous complaints. The most{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sheltered situations are the Boulevard Carabacel and the Quartiers Bran-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}colar and Cimiez, in the last of which the air is generally pure and free{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from dust. Three different climatic zones are distinguished and recom¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mended to different classes of patients, viz. the neighbourhood of the sea,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the plain, and the hills. Sunset is a critical period. As the sun disappears,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a sensation is often felt as if a damp mantle were being placed on the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}shoulders, but this moisture lasts 1-2 hours only. The rainy season usually{% endraw %}
{% raw %}begins early in October and lasts about a month. The dry, warm, and.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bracing climate of Nice is specially beneficial for chronic invalids, who{% endraw %}
{% raw %}are free from fever and pain, convalescents, and elderly people. The town{% endraw %}
{% raw %}also affords greater comfort and variety than any other place in the Ri¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}viera. — The result of the observations made at the Meteorological Station,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}established in 1877, are posted up on the band-kiosk in the Jardin Public.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Nice, Ital. Nizza, the capital (53,400inhab.) of the French De'-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}partement des Alpes Maritimes, was founded by the Phocian in¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}habitants of Marseilles in the 5th cent. B.C., and named Nicaea.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Down to 1388 it belonged to the County of Provence, and afterwards{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to the Dukes of Savoy; in 1792 it was occupied by the French, in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}1814 restored to Sardinia, and in 1860 finally annexed to France{% endraw %}
{% raw %}together with Savoy. Nice was the birthplace of the French general{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Massena (in 1758, d. 1817) and of Giuseppe Garibaldi (in 1807,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}d. 1882). The dialect of the natives is a mixture of Provencal and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Italian.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}In winter Nice is the rendezvous of invalids as well as persons{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in robust health from all parts of Europe, especially from England,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Russia, and Germany, who assemble here to escape from the rigours{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of a northern winter. In summer the town is deserted.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Nice is beautifully situated on the broad Baie des Anges, which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}opens towards the S., at the mouth of the Paglione, or Paillon{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(a small stream , frequently dried up). The broad and stony bed{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the river, with handsome quays on each bank, bisects the town.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}On the left bank is the Old Town , with its narrow , dirty lanes,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which however have been superseded by better streets near the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}shore" (Boulevard du Midi and Promenade du Cours). On the right{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bank is the Strangers' Quarter, which already surpasses the old{% endraw %}
{% raw %}town in extent, and is intended to occupy the entire space bounded{% endraw %}
{% raw %}on the W. by the brook Mognan, and on the N. by the railway{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(the Quartiers de la Croix de Marbre and des Beaumettes stretch{% endraw %}
{% raw %}along the coast to the W., the Boulevard Carabacel and the Quartiers{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Brancolar and Cimiez to the N.E. along the bank of the Paillon).{% endraw %}

{% raw %}lyptus Globulus). In the Avenue de la Gare, leading from the{% endraw %}
