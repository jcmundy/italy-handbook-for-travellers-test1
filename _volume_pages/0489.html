{% raw %}Cathedral.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Leaning Tower, the Baptistery, and the Campo Santo situated{% endraw %}
{% raw %}here, form a group of buildings without parallel, especially as it is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}situated beyond the precincts of the town and therefore removed{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from its disturbing influences.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The ** Cathedral (PI. 22), erected after the great naval victory{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the Pisans near Palermo (1063) by Busketus and Rainaldus in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Tuscan style, and consecrated by Pope Gelasius II. in 1118,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is a basilica with nave and double aisles, and transept flanked{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with aisles, 104 yds. in length, and 35!/2 yds. in breadth in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}interior, and covered with an elliptical dome over the centre. This{% endraw %}
{% raw %}remarkably perfect edifice is constructed entirely of white marble,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with black and coloured ornamentation. The most magnificent part{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is the facade, which in the lower story is adorned with columns{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and arches attached to the wall, and in the upper parts with four{% endraw %}
{% raw %}open galleries, gradually diminishing in length. The choir is also{% endraw %}
{% raw %}imposing. The ancient bronze-gates were replaced in 1602 by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the present doors, with representations of scriptural subjects,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}executed by Mocchi, Tacca, Mora, and others, from designs by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Giovanni da Bologna. The only one of the old doors now existing{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is the Crociera di S. Ranieri in the S. aisle, by Bonannus (12th{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cent.), representing 24 scriptural scenes.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}side, opposite the Campanile) is borne by 68 ancient Roman and Greek{% endraw %}
{% raw %}columns captured by the Pisans in war. (The capitals are now covered{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with stucco.) The nave has a flat coffered ceiling, richly gilded, the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}aisles are vaulted, and above them run triforia which cross the transept{% endraw %}
